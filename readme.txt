== Description ==

WP Decoratr can automatically find images relevant to your post content from Flickr to spice up your posts.

WP Decoratr is very easy to use and doesn't need any configuration. It will add a panel on the "Write Post" page. Compose your post as usual and click "decoratr" button on the editor toolbar when you're done. WP Decoratr will analyse your post content and suggest several matching images. Just click on an image to insert it in your post.

= Features =

1. Makes your posts eye catching - After all a picture is worth a thousand words.
2. No manual searching required  - WP Decoratr automatically finds the best images matching your content.
3. Easy to use - Just click an image to add it to your post.
4. Fast - WP Decoratr uses AJAX to return the results quickly.
5. No overhead - The plugin is very lightweight and and is only active on the "Write Post" page.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-decoratr` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Thats it! You will now have a "WP Decoratr" panel on the "Write Post" page.
